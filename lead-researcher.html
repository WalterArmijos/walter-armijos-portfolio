What it does

Research like a person, at machine speed

A list of leads is not a pipeline. This agent turns a qualified lead into something a closer can actually use.

RESEARCH

Deep audit

It studies the business the way a sharp rep would before a big call: the website, the reviews, the market position, what they sell and to whom.

DIAGNOSIS

Pain points, mapped

It identifies where the business is actually leaking money or missing demand, then correlates each gap with what your business could do about it.

OUTREACH

Copy that sounds human

No fake compliments, no filler, no AI voice. Straight to the point, grounded in what it found, written like someone with authority who understands the business.

The output

Outreach with no AI slop

Here is the kind of message it writes. Diagnostic, specific, and respectful of the reader's time. The business below is made up for demonstration.

To: Dana Pruitt, Owner, Harborline Roofing Co.
Subject: Harborline's website is capping your ticket size

Dana, your reviews say premium roofer. Your website says small operation. There is no quote capture and no financing option, and for a company doing your volume in Ozone Park, those two gaps are what cap average job value.

We fix exactly this for roofing companies: quote form, financing call to action, and the local search basics, live inside a week. Worth fifteen minutes?
