Who I am

I grew up in a family of builders. My father runs a roofing and construction company. My mother runs a cleaning business. I watched them operate without the tools that could have made their lives easier, and that stuck with me.

Today I work in B2B sales at Lev, a commercial real estate financing and AI platform in NYC. I prospect CRE sponsors, brokers, and operators and generate pipeline end to end. Before that I was a full-cycle BDR at Topline Pro, closing my own deals from day one.

On the side I co-run MVR Roofing and Esthela's Cleaning Service with my parents, and operate 6th Street Digital, my own web design and branding studio. I build software tools in my spare time because I cannot stop thinking about the problems around me.

I graduated from NYU Stern with a BS in Business.
