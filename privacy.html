Privacy

This is a personal portfolio site. It does not collect personal data and does not set cookies. The site uses Vercel Web Analytics, a cookieless service that counts page views anonymously and does not track you across sites.

A few third party services are involved when the site loads. Fonts are served by Google Fonts, and some project pages load a website preview image generated by WordPress mShots. Those services may receive your IP address as part of serving those files, the same as any image or font loaded from the web.

Links to email, LinkedIn, and external websites take you to services with their own privacy policies.

All sample business data shown in project demos (names, contacts, emails, phone numbers) is fictional and labeled as such.
